## Scope

- Short-lived Cloudflare Realtime TURN credentials are generated by the Worker.
- Long-term TURN key material remains only in Worker secrets.
- Credentials are delivered only through the authenticated room WebSocket.
- Existing peers receive refreshed ICE configuration and voice recovery is retried.
- Missing or unavailable TURN configuration falls back to direct P2P / STUN-only voice.
- Browser-problematic port 53 entries and non-Cloudflare ICE hosts are rejected.
- Protocol remains 6.

2. Set `TURN_KEY_ID` and `TURN_KEY_API_TOKEN` with `wrangler secret put` and redeploy.
3. Confirm two HTTPS clients show `TURN FALLBACK AVAILABLE`.
4. Test voice across different or restrictive networks.
5. Confirm no long-term TURN secret appears in source, browser payloads, logs, or console output.
6. Rotate/remove the secrets and confirm graceful STUN-only fallback.
7. Confirm frontend and Worker release manifests match.
